Artist, scientist, and inventor. PhD in Art History, member of the International Association for Shell and Spatial Structures (IASS) and the Synergetics Collaborative (USA). Co-founder of the European Society for Mathematics and Art.
            
He has lectured at Yale University and the Rhode Island School of Design. He has participated in many Bridges: Mathematics and Art exhibitions, Science Art shows in Moscow, and is a regular participant in Abstract Project exhibitions in Paris.
